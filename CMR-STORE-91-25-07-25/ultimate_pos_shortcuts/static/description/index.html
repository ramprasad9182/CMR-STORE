POS Keyboard Shortcuts

Introduction

Welcome to POS Keyboard Shortcuts, the ultimate tool for enhancing efficiency and speed at your point of sale. Our app introduces a seamless and intuitive way to manage sales transactions through keyboard shortcuts, reducing dependency on mouse clicks and touchscreens.

Why Choose POS Keyboard Shortcuts?

In today's fast-paced retail environment, efficiency is key. Traditional point of sale systems often rely heavily on touchscreens and mouse inputs, which can slow down the transaction process and lead to errors. POS Keyboard Shortcuts transforms your POS experience by allowing you to perform essential functions using only the keyboard, ensuring a faster, more efficient, and error-free workflow.

Usefull Features

Keyboard Shortcuts Helper

With the Keyboard Shortcuts Helper, you can easily view and learn all available shortcuts, making it simple to integrate them into your daily operations. This feature ensures that both new and experienced staff can quickly become proficient in using the shortcuts. Simply press Ctrl + Shift + ? to access the helper and start enhancing your productivity today.

Category Selector

The Category Selector allows you to quickly navigate through product categories using keyboard shortcuts. This feature speeds up the product selection process, making it easier to find and add items to a sale without relying on a mouse or touchscreen.
