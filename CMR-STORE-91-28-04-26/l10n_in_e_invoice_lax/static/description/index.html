E-Invoice for India

This module simplifies and automates the creation of electronic invoices and e-way bills for businesses registered under the GST Act in India. Integrated seamlessly into Odoo, it ensures compliance with GST regulations, making IRN generation and invoice management effortless.

Why E-Invoicing?

  • Legal Compliance: Required for businesses with turnover above ₹5 crore as per GST law.
  • Prevention of Tax Evasion: Real-time authentication reduces fake invoices.
  • Automation & Efficiency: Standardized format allows easy ERP integration.
  • Accurate ITC Claims: Auto-populated GSTR-1 prevents discrepancies.
  • Interoperability: Machine-readable invoices integrate with E-Way Bills & GST returns.
  • Global Standards: Uses Peppol standard, facilitating international compliance.
  • Business Efficiency: Faster processing, reduced reconciliation efforts.

How It Works

Step 1:

Navigate to Invoicing → Customers → Invoices and create a new invoice and confirm.

Step 2:

Confirm the invoice and click the Generate IRN button that Generates an electronic invoice and IRN, ensuring compliance with GST rules.

Step 3:

Success Message.

Step 4:

View the generated IRN details under the E-Invoice Details tab.

Step 5:

To cancel an IRN, click Cancel IRN and enter the reason in the popup.

Step 6:

Click Generate Cancel IRN to finalize cancellation. Details will be updated in the E-Invoice tab.

Step 7:

Within the invoice form view, under the Actions dropdown Print > Invoices
