E-Way Bill for India

This module simplifies and automates the creation of electronic E-Way Bills for businesses registered under the GST Act in India. Integrated with Odoo's Delivery Orders and Invoices, it ensures compliance and hassle-free transport documentation.

Why E-Way Bill ?

  • Legal Requirement: Mandatory for movement of goods valued above ₹50,000 as per GST law.
  • Transparency: Ensures clear tracking of goods movement and prevents tax evasion.
  • Efficiency: Reduces documentation and promotes faster movement of goods across states.
  • Compliance: Helps businesses stay compliant with GST rules during transportation.
  • Digitalization: Eliminates manual paperwork with an online system for real-time verification.

How It Works

Step 1:

Go to Inventory → Delivery Orders and select a transfer. Click the Create E-Way Bill/ Challan button in the transfer header.

Step 2:

Generate E-Way Bill that generate EWB no.

Step 3:

Generate Consolidated E-Way Bill. View the generated EWB details under the E-Way Bill Details tab.

Step 5:

To cancel an Eway bill, click Cancel and enter the reason in the popup.

Step 7:

Within the E-Way Bill form view, under the Actions dropdown Print > E-Way Bill Report
